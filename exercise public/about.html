About Me

I am a graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Njala University. Currently, I am preparing for several professional certifications, including CCNA, CEH, PMI, ITIL4, and CISSP, to further enhance my skills and expertise in the IT field

I was born in Sumbuya Lugbu, Southern Sierra Leone, and raised in Bo City, where I completed my primary and secondary education. My passion for technology led me to pursue higher education in Computer Science in Freetown.

Programming is more than just a career for me—it is a source of joy and continuous learning. Outside of my professional interests, I am an avid football fan, with Liverpool FC being my favorite team.

I am committed to growing my knowledge and skills to contribute effectively to the tech community and beyond.
